Ingredients

  • 1 Cup - Veganaise
  • 3 Cups - Roasted Red Peppers
  • 1/4 Cup - Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup - Sriracha
  • 4 Tbl - Sugar
  • 3 Tbl - Onion Powder
  • 3 Tbl - Garlic Powder
  • 3 Tbl - Cajun Seasoning
  • 3 Tbl - Kosher Salt
    •

Steps to take for Good Eatin'!

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth.
