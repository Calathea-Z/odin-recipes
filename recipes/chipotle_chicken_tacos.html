Chipotle Chicken Tacos

A complex yet simple dish of chicken thighs roasted in chipotle salsa

I learned this dish from a former colleague of mine while working in a Pizza joint of all places!
This spot had pretty good pizza but the real appeal were the stellar tacos. I love this recipe
because it is simple but so damn tasty!

Ingredients

  • 5 lb. Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Baby Teaspoon of salsa
