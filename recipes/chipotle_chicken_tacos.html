Chipotle Chicken Tacos

A complex yet simple dish of chicken thighs roasted in chipotle salsa

I learned this dish from my main man Mr. Loffer - a very good friened of mine. We worked together in a Pizza joint that specialized in Tacos... Go Figure (SMDH ;)


It's DAMN TASTY!

Ingredients

    For the Chicken Thighs

  • 5 lb - Chicken Thighs
  • 1/2 Cup - Vegetables Base
  • 4 Cups - Water
  • 2 Cups - Salsa
  • 1/4 Can - Chipotles in Adobo
  • 1/4 Cup - Chicken Seasoning
  • 1/2 Cup - Olive Oil

    • For the Tacos

  • 1 Pack - Corn or Flour tortillas (do your self a favor and just choose corn)
  • 1 Bunch Cilantro - Coarsly Chopped
  • 2 Cups - Pico de Gallo
  • 1 Cup - Baja Sauce
  • 1/8 Cup - Pickled Red Onions
    •

Steps to Take for Good Eatin'!

Steps for Chicken Preperation

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Place chicken thighs in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add oil and Secret Chicken Seasoning to the bowl and thoroughly rub into chicken thighs.
  4. Sear the chicken thighs over high heat for 5 minutes on each side and then add them to a deep baking pan.
  5. Using a blender mix together the water, chiptoles in adobo, salsa, and vegetable stock
  6. Pour the salsa mixture into the pan with the chicken being careful to cover as much of the chicken as possible with the liquid.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until the interal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  8. Take the chicken out of the oven and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  9. Roughly chop the chicken to taco-sized bites.

Steps for Taco Assembly! DONT FUCK THIS UP :)

  1. Warm the tortillas in a pan with a bit of olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken to the tortilla followed by the pico de gallo.
  3. Next add the pickled red onions and the baja sauce.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve open face.
